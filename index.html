Meet the Founder

If you've ever left home wondering whether your dog is truly okay, I built this for you.

I've been caring for pets for most of my life. I started my first pet sitting business at 12 years old, worked as a dog walker in college, and have always believed great pet care is about more than feeding schedules and walks. It's about trust.

As a dog owner myself, I know how hard it can be to truly relax when you're away from home. Too often, I found myself wondering whether my dogs were in good hands.

That's why I created Port City Leash Club.

By combining a lifelong love of animals with years of experience building teams and customer-focused businesses, my goal is simple: to create the most trusted pet care experience in Wilmington, built on reliability, communication, and genuine relationships.

Alison Reilley
Founder, Port City Leash Club
