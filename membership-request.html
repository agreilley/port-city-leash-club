Set up recurring weekly walks with a consistent schedule and a team you can trust. Tell us about your dog, choose your preferred routine, and request your complimentary meet & greet.

Every membership begins with a complimentary meet & greet.

We'll take time to learn your dog's routine, personality, home access details, and preferences before the first walk.

Step 01

Choose your membership

Select the recurring walk plan that best fits your weekly routine. Your estimated monthly investment will update as you choose your plan and days.

Step 02

Choose your schedule

Select your preferred start date, walking days, and time window. Daily memberships automatically include Monday through Friday.

Need to make a change? Upcoming walks can be managed through the member portal. Schedule changes must be made at least 48 hours in advance.

Step 03

Tell us about your dog

Basic information to help us get to know your dog before your meet & greet.

Step 04

Your information

We'll use this to confirm your service area, follow up on your request, and prepare for your meet & greet.

Step 05

Schedule your complimentary meet & greet

Choose an available time for us to meet you and your dog, review your routine, and confirm next steps before recurring walks begin.

What happens next
  1. We review your membership request and confirm availability in your neighborhood.
  2. We confirm your preferred schedule and membership fit.
  3. We meet you and your dog for a complimentary meet & greet.
  4. Once approved, your recurring walks begin.

Submitting this request does not create a paid membership yet. We'll confirm details with you before recurring care begins.
